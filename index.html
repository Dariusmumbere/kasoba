About

Stories Rooted
in Real Life

"Film is a powerful tool for preserving culture, inspiring hope, and giving voice to overlooked communities."

Kasoba Godwin is a Ugandan filmmaker, producer, director, screenwriter, and actor whose work reflects a deep commitment to authentic storytelling and social impact. With over 9 years of experience and more than 11 completed projects, he has built a diverse and dynamic portfolio across multiple roles in the film industry.

He is the founder and head of SaintHood Film Production, based in Kasese, Uganda — dedicated to developing compelling stories and nurturing local talent within the region's growing film industry.

His films have received recognition at regional and international levels, including official selections in festivals across Uganda, the United Kingdom, and internationally.
